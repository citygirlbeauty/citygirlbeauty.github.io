How to do the perfect winged eyeliner

  • Tip For Beginners: Rest your elbow on a stable surface when doing your eyeliner, it'll make it more easier to apply eyeliner & create the wing
  • We are going to begin in the middle section of your eyelid
  • From the middle, draw a diagonal line to the end your eye
  • Once you reach the end of your eyelid, you must stretch your eye a little to be able to draw your winged angle at the end
  • Go back to the middle part of your eyelid, and draw straight diagonal line (to the length of your choice). Make it match to the end of your eyebrow bone
  • Now just fill in that outline and don't forget to fill in also your inner part of your eye (innermost corner)
    • And your done (:

Recommendations

To create that perfect contour line, fill in your eyebrows, apply eyeshadow to base and crease area on eyes, and to apply foundation to your face. I recommend using Real Techniques ™ Brushes !
